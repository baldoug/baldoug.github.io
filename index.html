I listen to have a nice life every day

Because I don't have a nice life.

An 85 minute journey through emotional nadirs that draws upon the sounds of 80s post-punk, shoegaze, drone, post-rock, industrial, gothic rock, black metal, ambient and proto-doom.
Recorded on a shoestring budget and released without fanfare in 2008, it quickly garnered a cult following and appeared on many album of the year and album of the decade lists.
